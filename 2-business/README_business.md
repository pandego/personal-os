# Business Domain

This area is for business-facing work that should stay separate from personal reflection and public content.

## What goes here

- `01-portfolio/` - portfolio assets, proof, and supporting material
- `02-outbound/` - outreach, newsletters, and business development material
- `03-clients/` - client-specific workspaces

## Skills that may matter here

- `get-started` - shape the system if the workspace is still fresh
- `review` - reflect on business progress during weekly or yearly reviews

## Keep it simple

Put business work here when it supports consulting, positioning, outreach, or client delivery.
